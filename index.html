Profile

Kenneth Kabiito is a handsome intelligent 27-year old Ugandan, currently a student at Makerere University, College of Computing and Information sciences, pursuing a bachelors degree in Computer Science. He is a brilliant, innovative and results oriented person capable of outside-the-box thinking to create solutions to recurring problems. Kenneth is more of a scientist than an artist, as he loves, studies and practices basic and advanced sciences in his day-to-day life.


He was born and grew up in Kireka, a Kampala suburb, for most of his childhood. He also studied in pretty-much the same area, for his nursery, primary, secondary education. Only for his University studies has he had to venture out to Kampala (Makerere University). He aspires to become a leading software developer for mobile and web apps, and also for robotics.
